KNOW ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

FCRF's Pioneering Role in Ensuring a Cyber-Safe India

Future Crime Research Foundation (FCRF) is an IIT Kanpur incubated, Section-8 non-profit organisation dedicated to advancing research, innovation, and capacity building in Cyber Security, Digital Crime, Fraud Risk Management, Cyber Laws and Cyber Forensics. Registered under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961, FCRF works at the intersection of technology, governance, and law to strengthen India’s digital resilience.

As one of India’s fastest-growing cyber research and skilling ecosystems, FCRF is committed to creating a digitally empowered society. Our mission is to decode emerging cyber threats, build strategic knowledge, and equip government bodies, industries, academic institutions, and citizens with the capabilities required to navigate an increasingly complex digital landscape.

Through deep research, public-private collaboration, policy engagement, high-impact training programs, and national awareness initiatives, FCRF is helping India transition into a secure, trusted, and future-ready digital nation. We focus on knowledge sharing, skill development, and customised cyber-risk solutions that address the unique needs of every stakeholder in society.

  • Digital Leadership for India FCRF envisions India as a global leader in the digital space by strengthening readiness across critical infrastructure, businesses, institutions, and society.
  • Future-Ready Cyber Ecosystem We work to build a strong, innovative, and multi-disciplinary ecosystem capable of addressing emerging cybersecurity and privacy challenges.
  • Nationwide Impact & Collaboration FCRF supports the Centre, State governments, industries, and individuals with research-driven insights and solutions to counter future digital threats.
