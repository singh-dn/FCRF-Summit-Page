KNOW ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Building a Cyber-Safe & Future-Ready India

Future Crime Research Foundation (FCRF) is an IIT Kanpur–incubated, non-profit organization dedicated to advancing research, awareness, and capacity building in cybersecurity, digital crime, and cyber forensics. We work closely with governments, industries, institutions, and society to address emerging digital threats and strengthen cyber resilience.

  • Conduct in-depth research on emerging cyber threats, digital crime trends, and technology-driven risks.
  • Build cyber capacity and awareness through conferences, workshops, training programs, and public outreach initiatives.
  • Deliver customized cyber and digital solutions to support secure digital transformation across sectors.

Award Categories

Explore the two nomination tracks under the FCRF Excellence Awards 2026.

Professional Awards
For leaders, experts and institutions

For professionals, risk leaders, and innovators securing India’s digital trust ecosystem.

Policing Awards
For officers, units and police institutions

Recognizing law enforcement excellence in cybercrime investigation, digital forensics, and public cyber safety.

What defines us

Integrity, impact and excellence in the fight against technology-enabled crime.

The FCRF Excellence Awards celebrate work that goes beyond titles and designations. They recognise measurable contribution, public impact, innovation, investigation excellence and leadership across India’s cyber, policing, legal, risk and digital trust ecosystem. Each category is designed to spotlight those who are building safer digital systems, stronger institutions and more resilient responses to emerging crimes.
