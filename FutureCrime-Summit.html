KNOW ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Building a Cyber-Safe & Future-Ready India

Future Crime Research Foundation (FCRF) is an IIT Kanpur–incubated, non-profit organization dedicated to advancing research, awareness, and capacity building in cybersecurity, digital crime, and cyber forensics. We work closely with governments, industries, institutions, and society to address emerging digital threats and strengthen cyber resilience.

  • Conduct in-depth research on emerging cyber threats, digital crime trends, and technology-driven risks.
  • Build cyber capacity and awareness through conferences, workshops, training programs, and public outreach initiatives.
  • Deliver customized cyber and digital solutions to support secure digital transformation across sectors.

KNOW ABOUT OUR FCRF

Researching Tomorrow’s Threats, Securing Today

Future Crime Research Foundation (FCRF) is an IIT Kanpur–incubated AIIDE-CoE Start-Up (Non-Profit NGO) dedicated to advancing India’s cybersecurity and digital-risk ecosystem. We specialise in Cyber Security, Digital Crime, Fraud Risk Management, Cyber Laws, Cyber Forensics, and Policy Research.

FCRF is registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 and Section 12A & 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Leadership in Cyber Innovation

Fully committed to strengthening India’s digital future through research, training, capacity-building, and national-level collaborations.

  • Turning complex cyber challenges into actionable intelligence for India’s digital safety.

  • Transforming cybersecurity awareness into resilience, readiness, and national capability.
